# IT Compliance Tracker Dashboard

> **FWD IT Internship Portfolio Project** · Nguyen Huu Anh · 2026

An interactive dashboard to track and monitor key IT & AI compliance regulations relevant to the insurance industry in Vietnam and globally.

**🔗 Live Demo:** [GitHub Pages](https://huuanh20.github.io/it-compliance-tracker)
**👤 Author:** [Nguyen Huu Anh](https://github.com/huuanh20)

---

## 🎯 Project Overview

| JD Requirement | What I Built |
|---|---|
| *"Track key IT compliance/regulatory updates"* | Full regulation dashboard with status tracking |
| *"Summarize what matters to the team"* | Summary cards + impact notes per regulation |
| *"Support data governance work"* | Control mapping & evidence checklist per reg |
| *"Research industry trends and best practices"* | Regulations sourced from EU, Vietnam, ISO standards |

---

## ✨ Features

- 📊 **Dashboard overview** — Active, Upcoming, Monitoring counts at a glance
- 🔍 **Filter by category** — AI, Data Privacy, Cybersecurity, Financial
- 🔎 **Search bar** — Instantly find any regulation
- 📅 **Deadline tracking** — Color-coded urgency indicators
- 📋 **Detail view** — Click any regulation for full summary + action items
- 🏢 **Insurance-specific** — Focused on regulations affecting FWD/insurance sector

---

## 📚 Regulations Covered

- EU AI Act (2024/2026)
- GDPR & Vietnam PDPD
- Vietnam Cybersecurity Law 2018
- ISO/IEC 27001 (InfoSec)
- NIST AI RMF
- Vietnam Digital Transformation Decree
- PCI-DSS (Payment Card)
- ISO/IEC 42001 (AI Management)
